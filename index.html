04 — About

The person behind the timeline.

"The difference between good and unforgettable lives in the details most people never notice."

I believe every frame carries weight. Not just visually, but emotionally. A well-placed cut can make someone hold their breath. The right color grade can turn noon into nostalgia.

I've spent years refining my craft — not chasing trends, but developing a sensitivity to rhythm, pacing, and the invisible architecture of a story. I work slowly. I work intentionally. Because the work that matters most is the work you feel without seeing.

When I'm not editing, I'm studying films, experimenting with new techniques, or finding stories worth telling. Every project I take on gets my full attention — because that's the only way I know how to work.
